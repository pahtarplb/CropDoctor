Appendix

Our Journey to Smart Crop Doctor

💡

First Insights - Awakening

Being from an agricultural family, witnessed sufferings of farmers and farm losses due to lack of timely information & disease detection. This ignited the concept of Smart Crop Doctor.

🔍

Identifying the Problem

Through extensive outreach, identified significant crop losses from diseases, pests & insect infestations - leading to development of an AI-driven diagnostic tool for Precision Agriculture.

🤖

The Solution

Smart Crop Doctor leverages AI & drone technology to monitor soil and plant health, enable early detection of pest attacks & diseases - helping farmers reduce losses & adopt sustainable practices.

💡 A ONE STOP SOLUTION providing farmers timely information, advisories, and loss prevention - bringing hope, happiness, and security.
