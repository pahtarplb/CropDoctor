The Problem

Indian farmers lose ₹90,000 Cr every year to crop diseases.

They detect problems too late. They spray too much. They have no data. And middlemen take 40% of what's left.

  • 🦟 20-30% crops lost to pests & disease annually
  • 🧪 40% chemicals wasted on wrong areas
  • 👁 Zero early warning systems available

Current Solutions

Today's options don't work.

Manual Inspection

Walk fields daily. Miss 80% of problems. Find disease only when visible - already too late.

Agri Advisory Apps

Generic tips. No field-specific data. Can't detect what's actually happening on your farm.

Government Extension

1 officer per 1,000 farmers. Visits once a season. Advice arrives after harvest.

Expensive Drones

₹10L+ to buy. Need trained pilots. No farmer can afford it individually.
